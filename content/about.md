      <h2>Following the connections between ice, ocean, and margins.</h2>
      <p>My research centers on Arctic Ocean dynamics and the use of trace elements and nutrients as indicators of circulation and biogeochemical processes. I am especially interested in how sea ice, rivers, sediments, and broad continental shelves influence the chemistry of the open ocean.</p>
      <p>That work has taken me from the Northern Gulf of Mexico and equatorial Pacific to the Arctic Ocean, landfast ice near Utqiaġvik, and the Amundsen Sea. Across settings, I value careful sampling, transparent analysis, reusable scientific tools, and collaboration across disciplines.</p>
