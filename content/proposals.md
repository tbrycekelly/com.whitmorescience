    <p class="ws-eyebrow">How this page works</p>
    <h2>Connecting ideas to outcomes</h2>
    <p>Proposal records are organized by funding status and linked to their resulting project pages and public research products. Only materials suitable for public release should be posted; confidential reviews, sensitive budget details, and collaborator documents should remain private.</p>

    <p class="ws-eyebrow">Unfunded and archived</p>
    <h2>Ideas worth preserving</h2>
    <p>Not every strong proposal is funded. Public summaries can preserve useful questions, document intellectual development, identify concepts suitable for revision, and connect later outputs back to their origins.</p>
