    <h2>New analytical infrastructure for Alaska</h2>
    <p>This Major Research Instrumentation award supports acquisition of a Thermo Scientific Element XR high-resolution inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometer. The instrument will enable precise elemental and isotope-ratio measurements at low concentrations across polar and global environmental applications.</p>
